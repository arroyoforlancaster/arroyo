Statement of Ownership and Management<\/strong>
\nThe following social media accounts are paid for, managed, and owned by Friends of Jaime Arroyo. They are not official publications of the City of Lancaster, PA.<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Facebook – https:\/\/www.facebook.com\/ArroyoforLancaster<\/li>\n
  • Instagram – https:\/\/www.instagram.com\/CouncilorArroyo<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Comment Moderation<\/strong>
    \nComments are moderated to ensure broad input and discussion. However, Friends of Jaime Arroyo may remove content it deems inappropriate including but not limited to comments, posts, or messages that contain:<\/p>\n

      \n
    • Offensive or violent language;<\/li>\n
    • Hateful or discriminatory comments regarding race, ethnicity, religion, gender, disability, sexual orientation or political beliefs;<\/li>\n
    • Links or comments containing sexually explicit content material;<\/li>\n
    • Violations of copyright or intellectual property rights;<\/li>\n
    • Spam, link baiting or files containing viruses that could damage the operation of other people\u2019s computers or mobile devices;<\/li>\n
    • Attacks on specific groups or any comments meant to harass, threaten or abuse an individual;<\/li>\n
    • False or misleading medical or legal information;<\/li>\n
    • Commercial solicitations.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
